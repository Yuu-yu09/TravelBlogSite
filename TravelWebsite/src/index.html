Popular Destinations

Alona Beach Resort

Alona Beach on Panglao Island, Bohol, felt like paradise with its pristine waters, fine white sand, and serene yet convenient location. The stunning sunsets, vibrant underwater life, and welcoming beachside resorts made it a truly unforgettable experience!

Taal Volcano

The view of Taal Volcano features its iconic crater island surrounded by the deep blue waters of Taal Lake, set against distant mountains under a partly cloudy sky. The volcano's rugged, ash-colored terrain contrasts beautifully with the lush greenery in the foreground, creating a stunning natural landscape.

BGC

BGC (Bonifacio Global City) is a top destination for locals and tourists, known for its modern vibe, upscale shopping malls, trendy restaurants, lively nightlife, well-planned streets, public art, and green spaces.

Daranak Falls

Daranak Falls, located in Tanay, Rizal, is popular for its natural beauty, featuring a cascading waterfall surrounded by lush greenery and clear, refreshing scene making it a favorite escape for nature lovers, picnics, and day trips.

Travel Tips

💼 Pack Light: Avoid overpacking by choosing versatile clothing.

💳 Budget Wisely: Use no-fee cards and track your expenses.

🛡️ Stay Safe: Always keep a digital copy of your documents.

🌍 Embrace Local Culture: Learn a few phrases in the local language.

🌿 Respect Nature: Follow 'Leave No Trace' principles when hiking.

🎒 Use Carry-Ons: Save on baggage fees and avoid lost luggage.
